# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for considering contributing to the UMich Reflection Rooms Map! Here are some guidelines to ensure a smooth contribution process:

## Getting Started
1. **Fork the repository** to your own GitHub account.
2. **Clone your forked repository** to your local machine.
3. **Create a new branch** for your feature or fix: `git checkout -b feature-your-feature-name`.

## Making Changes
- Make your desired changes in the codebase.
- Ensure your code is clean and follows the project's coding standards.
- Write clear commit messages describing your changes.

## Testing Your Changes
- Test your changes locally to ensure everything is functioning as expected.
- Include any necessary tests for new features or fixes.

## Submitting Your Changes
- Push your changes to your forked repository: `git push origin feature-your-feature-name`.
- Go to the original repository and submit a **pull request** detailing your changes and why they should be merged.

## Code Review
- Be open to feedback on your pull request. Collaborate with the maintainers to improve your contribution if necessary.

## Additional Resources
- Refer to the [GitHub Docs](https://docs.github.com/en) for more information on how to use GitHub.
- Join our Discord channel for real-time discussions and assistance with your contributions.

We appreciate your contributions and look forward to collaborating with you!
